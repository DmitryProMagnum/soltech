Cookie Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Privacy Policy Cookie Policy Terms & Disclaimer

This policy explains how soltech.solutions uses cookies. In short: we keep it minimal. We use only the cookies strictly necessary to serve and protect the site — no analytics, no advertising, no tracking.

1What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make sites work, to keep them secure, and — on many sites — to measure and advertise. Similar technologies (such as local storage) work in comparable ways.

2Our approach

SolTech is built to be privacy-first. We do not use analytics, advertising, marketing or social-media tracking cookies, and we do not sell or share cookie data with third parties for advertising. We do not profile you based on your browsing.

3Cookies used on this site

The only cookies that may be set are strictly-necessary ones placed by our hosting and security provider to keep the site available and protect it from malicious traffic:

CookieSet byPurposeTypeDuration
__cf_bm / cf_clearance (and similar) Cloudflare — our host & CDN Distinguishes real visitors from bots and protects the site against attacks and abuse. Strictly necessary Session to ~30 minutes

These are required for the website to operate securely. Under the PDPL and common practice, strictly-necessary cookies do not require your prior consent.

4When you leave our site

Our pages include links to WhatsApp and email. When you click them you leave soltech.solutions, and the destination service (for example Meta/WhatsApp, or your email provider) may set its own cookies under its own policy. We have no control over those cookies.

5Why there is no cookie pop-up

Because we use only strictly-necessary cookies and no tracking, we do not interrupt you with a cookie consent banner. If we ever introduce analytics or marketing cookies in future, we will first add a consent mechanism that lets you accept or decline them, and we will update this page before doing so.

6Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings — every major browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge) lets you view, block or clear them. Note that blocking strictly-necessary cookies may affect the site's security features. Guidance for common browsers is available at aboutcookies.org.

7Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if our use of cookies changes. The "last updated" date above shows the current version.

8Contact us

Questions about cookies? Email hello@soltech.solutions.

Please note: this policy describes the cookies used at the time of writing. The exact strictly-necessary cookies set by Cloudflare can change; if you later add any third-party tools (analytics, chat widgets, embedded maps or videos, ad pixels), this page and your consent approach must be updated accordingly.
