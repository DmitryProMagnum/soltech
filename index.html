Commercial models

Three ways to pay — all with zero CapEx

Every model is fully funded and managed by SolTech. We recommend the structure that fits your treasury and your site.

Most common
Shared savings

Pay a share of what you save

You pay SolTech a fixed share of the verified savings — and nothing more. If the system produces less, you pay less. The strongest fit for large sites, and the model with the most aligned incentives.

Fixed service fee

A predictable monthly fee

A flat monthly charge, set below the DEWA cost it replaces. Easiest to budget — ideal for multinationals and groups that want a single, fixed line item.

Hybrid

A small base plus a share

A modest fixed payment plus a share of the savings. The most balanced option — it lowers SolTech's risk while keeping both sides focused on maximum output.
