How we choose

Hardware picked for your numbers, not our margins

DEWA's Shams Dubai programme only permits equipment held on its online Eligible Equipment registry — a live list of approved panels and inverters that is updated continuously rather than published as a fixed document. We treat that registry as the floor, not the finish line: every component we propose sits on it, and then we narrow further on how the hardware performs and pays back in Dubai’s climate.

An important detail shapes how we work. DEWA approves specific models, not whole brands. A manufacturer being well known does not, on its own, make a given panel or inverter eligible. So while this page groups the makers we most often specify, the exact listed model for your roof is confirmed at the design stage — matched to your load, your shading and the current registry — and set out in your proposal before anything is ordered.

You are welcome to read DEWA’s own material on the Shams Dubai programme. Everything we install is designed to clear its approval and connection process cleanly.

What we look for

Four tests every component has to pass

The same criteria apply whether it is a villa rooftop or a warehouse array. They protect your yield and your warranty for the full life of the system.

01

Yield in real heat

Dubai rooftops run hot, and a panel’s rated output is measured in a lab. We favour modules with a low temperature coefficient that hold their output through summer, so the system delivers close to its modelled ~1,750 kWh per kWp each year.

02

Warranty & bankability

Panels typically carry 25–30 year product and performance warranties; inverters 5–10 years. A warranty is only as good as the maker behind it, so we weigh the manufacturer’s track record and financial standing — its bankability — not just the paper.

03

On DEWA’s registry

The exact model has to appear on DEWA’s Eligible Equipment list at the time we file. We confirm the listing per project, because the registry changes and eligibility is decided model by model, never by brand alone.

04

Serviceable in the UAE

Spare parts, firmware and warranty support need to be reachable locally. We specify equipment with an established presence in the UAE so that, years from now, a replacement inverter or a claim is straightforward rather than an import project.
