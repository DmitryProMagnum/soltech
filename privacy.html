SolTech respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect when you use soltech.solutions or contact us, why we collect it, who we share it with, and the rights you have under the UAE Personal Data Protection Law.

1Who we are

This website is operated by SolTech, a solar-panel installation and maintenance business operating under DEWA's Shams Dubai programme in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

  • Legal entity: [registered company name]
  • Trade licence No.: [trade licence number]
  • Registered office: [registered address, Dubai, UAE]
  • Contact for privacy matters: hello@soltech.solutions

For the purposes of the UAE Personal Data Protection Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021, the "PDPL"), SolTech is the "Controller" of your personal data.

2What data we collect

Information you give us

When you contact us by WhatsApp or email, or request a free engineering assessment, you may provide: your name, phone number, email address, property location, your average monthly DEWA bill or DEWA account details, and information about your roof or site that you choose to share.

Information collected automatically

Our hosting and security provider (Cloudflare) records standard technical logs — such as your IP address, browser type, the pages requested and timestamps — to deliver the site and protect it against malicious traffic. We do not use analytics, advertising or tracking tools, and we do not build profiles of visitors.

What we do not collect

We do not knowingly collect data from children, and we do not ask for sensitive/special-category data (such as health, religion or biometric data) through this website.

3The savings calculator

The savings calculator runs entirely in your browser. The bill amount you type is used only to display an on-screen estimate — it is not transmitted to us or stored on our servers unless you deliberately send it to us (for example, in a WhatsApp message).

4How we use your data & our legal basis

Under the PDPL we process personal data only where we have a lawful basis to do so:

  • To answer your enquiry and prepare a savings pre-calculation — at your request / to take steps toward a contract.
  • To design your system, prepare a proposal and file your application with DEWA under the Shams Dubai programme — to perform our contract with you and with your consent.
  • To operate, secure and maintain the website — our legitimate interest in running a safe, reliable service.
  • To send you service messages you asked for — with your consent, which you may withdraw at any time.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for automated decision-making that produces legal effects concerning you.

5Who we share it with

  • DEWA — when you engage us for an installation, we submit the details needed to obtain approval and connection for your grid-tied system under the Shams Dubai programme.
  • Service providers — our website host and security provider (Cloudflare), and the messaging channels you choose to use (for example WhatsApp / Meta when you message us there), each governed by their own privacy terms.
  • Authorities — where we are required to disclose information by UAE law or lawful request.

We never share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

6International transfers

Some of our providers (for example Cloudflare and Meta/WhatsApp) may process data on servers outside the UAE. Where this happens, the transfer is made because it is necessary to provide the service you asked for and, where relevant, with your consent — relying on the transfer conditions permitted under the PDPL and on the contractual safeguards those providers maintain.

7How long we keep it

We keep enquiry data only for as long as needed to respond to you. If you become a customer, we keep your records for the life of your system and its warranty, plus any period required by UAE tax and commercial law. After that, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

8How we protect it

The site is served over encrypted HTTPS, and we apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against loss, misuse and unauthorised access. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9Your rights

Subject to the conditions in the PDPL, you have the right to: access the data we hold about you; have it corrected or completed; have it erased; restrict or object to how we process it; withdraw a consent you gave us; and request a copy of certain data in a portable format. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UAE Data Office.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@soltech.solutions. We will respond within the timeframe required by law and may need to verify your identity first.

10Cookies

This website uses only strictly-necessary cookies. For details, see our Cookie Policy.

11Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top shows when it was last revised; significant changes will be reflected on this page.

12Contact us

Questions about this policy or your personal data? Email hello@soltech.solutions and we'll be glad to help.
