How you pay

Three ways to structure it

Every model is No-CapEx and leaves you paying less than the grid — they differ only in how the savings are shared and how much of your cost is fixed. We tune the choice to your load profile and appetite for predictability.

Most chosen
Model 01

Shared savings

You pay nothing up front and nothing fixed. Your solar cost tracks what the system actually generates against your DEWA bill, and the resulting savings are shared — so our return depends entirely on your output. Aligned incentives, zero downside.

Model 02

Fixed service fee

A flat monthly fee for the system, locked for the contract term. Wholly predictable and easy to budget — and because it is fixed, your solar cost stays put while DEWA tariffs keep climbing.

Model 03

Hybrid

A modest fixed base plus a performance-linked share. Blends the certainty of a fixed fee with the upside of shared savings — calibrated to your consumption pattern and how you prefer to carry risk.

Systems are grid-tied under DEWA’s Shams Dubai programme: they lower your daytime energy cost rather than providing backup during an outage. Where continuity matters, battery storage can be designed in.
