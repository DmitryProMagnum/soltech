The investment thesis

Real assets, contracted income, a market with a tailwind

The case is deliberately simple. Physical infrastructure you can point to, paid for by energy a business would have bought anyway, in a market where demand is structurally rising. Not a bet on a technology cycle — ownership of the plant that meets the load.

Hard, real assets

Every project is physical plant — solar and on-site energy infrastructure installed on commercial rooftops and sites, with a productive life of 5 years and more. Tangible, insured, warranted assets, not paper exposure.

Contracted, long-term cash flows

Income comes from multi-year contracts with creditworthy commercial offtakers — warehouses, manufacturers, cold storage, data centres, hotels. They pay for power they would buy from the grid regardless, at a lower cost, so the offtake is real and the incentive to keep paying is built in.

Structural demand growth

UAE electricity demand keeps rising with population, industry and cooling load, while national Net Zero 2050 and clean-energy targets pull generation on-site. The pipeline sits on a long-term tailwind, not a subsidy cycle.

How the model works

SolTech builds and runs the asset. You are the capital partner

Businesses take on our systems with no capital outlay — SolTech funds, owns and operates them. That upfront capital is where investors come in: you fund the assets, and the long-term contracts return the cash flow they produce.

01

Origination

We source and qualify commercial and industrial sites with high daytime load and sound offtaker credit — the profile that makes on-site energy work for everyone.

02

Capital & structuring

Investor capital funds the build. Each asset is structured under a defined long-term offtake contract, with terms, tenor and risk allocation set out before a panel is fitted.

03

Design & build

Engineered, DEWA-approved under Shams Dubai, installed and grid-connected turnkey by one team — so the asset is commissioned to specification and to schedule.

04

Long-term contract

The client pays a monthly amount below the DEWA power it replaces — shared savings, fixed service fee or a hybrid — locked in for years. Their bill falls from month one.

05

Operate & maintain

SolTech monitors, cleans, insures and warranties the asset for its life, holding output near target — because performance is what protects the cash flow.

06

Contracted returns

The contracted payments flow back to the capital, net of operations — asset-backed income over the life of the contract, subject to terms.

One operator

One team owns the whole asset — and its performance

Across the portfolio, SolTech is a single accountable operator. We originate the project, coordinate its financing, engineer and build it, secure DEWA approvals, then monitor, clean, insure and manage the warranties for its life. For an investor that means one counterparty responsible for the assets — not a loose chain of subcontractors passing risk between them.

End to end, one operator handles:

  • Origination & underwriting — site selection, load analysis and offtaker credit.
  • Engineering & construction — design, DEWA approval, installation and grid connection.
  • Contract management — shared-savings, service-fee and hybrid agreements over their full term.
  • Operations & maintenance — continuous monitoring, cleaning, insurance and warranty claims.
  • Performance reporting — transparent, ongoing reporting on output and contracted cash flow.

Alignment is the point. The same discipline that guarantees a client’s bill falls from month one — keeping systems producing at their best — is exactly what protects the contracted cash flow behind the asset. Client, operator and investor all want the same thing: maximum output, for the life of the contract.
